About

Science deserves a
creative partner.

Proper Motion was founded on the belief that the most important ideas in the world are trapped inside datasets, papers, and labs — and that the right creative technology can set them free.

We work at the intersection of design, engineering, and scientific research to build experiences that don't just inform — they shift perspective. Our collaborations span immersive audio, interactive data visualization, installations, and experimental media.

Our Approach

The Proper Motion Method

01

Insight Through Interaction

Understanding emerges through interaction, experimentation, and exploration — not passive observation. We design systems where the act of engaging is the act of learning.

02

Embodied Understanding

Complex data becomes intuitive through spatial, visual, and auditory experience working together. We translate the unreachable — light-years, centuries, spectral lines — into something you can stand inside.

03

Dynamic Discovery

We don't design fixed narratives. We design for open-ended exploration where the path is shaped by curiosity — the user's and the scientist's alike.
